Why Chinese Medicine

In the Chinese Medicine methods, we analyze the whole body balance instead of focusing on only the individual symptoms. By using herbal medicine, acupuncture and other natural treatment methods, we aim to restore the whole body balance thus your body regain the inner power to protect, heal and defend itself.

Yin & Yang

Excess & Deficiency

Interior & Exterior

In Chinese medicine, every individual is different. The same symptom can come from different root problems. For example, insomnia can be caused by liver qi stagnation, heart fire flaring, kidney yin deficiency, or heat and dampness accumulation. Analyzing each person’s unique situation with the lens of Yin & Yang, Hot & Cold, Excess & Deficiency, Interior & Exterior (traditional eight-principle diagnosis), we come up with a treatment plan that is customized and effective. In the treatment plan, herbs are used for their different properties to compensate for the body’s needs, and acupuncture stimulates meridians to promote a smooth energy flow.
